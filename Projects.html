Description
________________________________________________________________________________
This is an operating system we are working on; as the name suggests, 
it's not going to be anything professional for a while. Speaking of the name, 
it holds a few Easter eggs related to the same topic; when you look at it face-on, it just says "TrashCan OS," 
but when you pay attention to the name, you get "trash can operating system," 
meaning that even trash can operate, albeit systemically, then, if you pay more attention, you notice that it also says "trash can Operate," 
removing the "system" and "ing"; aside from the name having many meanings, 
this OS will be text-based, small, and will have many built-in programs to keep the user entertained.
